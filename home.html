ABOUT "HOPE FOR HUMANITY"

The Hope For Humanity has granted wishes that have given hope to thousands of children, youth and their families since 20__. We provide funding to schools and teachers to help students stay engaged and thrive in school. Community organizations count on our support and funding for their programs that help underprivileged children and youth. In addition, we also provide acts of hope through funding to children and families that need immediate support. .
